# Toblen Stonehill

**Race/Ancestry:** Human
**Occupation:** Innkeeper
**Location:** Stonehill Inn, Phandalin
**Faction:** None (concerned citizen)
**Status:** Alive

## Appearance

A short, friendly young human male with the welcoming demeanor of a natural host. He has the look of someone who works hard and takes pride in his establishment.

## Personality

- **Trait:** Friendly and welcoming; genuinely enjoys hosting travelers
- **Ideal:** Community - A thriving inn means a thriving town
- **Bond:** Deep love for his wife Trilena and son Pip
- **Flaw:** Risk-averse; fears Redbrand retaliation against his family

## Voice/Mannerisms

Speaks warmly and engagingly. Eager to share news and gossip. Voice drops when discussing the Redbrands. Glances nervously at the door when sensitive topics arise.

## Background

Toblen is a native of Triboar, a larger town to the east. He came to Phandalin hoping to make his fortune as a prospector. He quickly realized he knew far more about running an inn than mining, so he established the Stonehill Inn with his wife Trilena.

The inn has become the social hub of Phandalin, where locals and travelers gather to share news, conduct business, and relax.

## Relationship to Party

- **First Met:** Stonehill Inn (likely first stop in Phandalin)
- **Disposition:** Friendly and helpful
- **Owes/Owed:** Standard innkeeper relationship; grateful if Redbrands are dealt with

## What They Know

- Local gossip and rumors (excellent source of information)
- The Redbrands have been terrorizing the town
- Harbin Wester (townmaster) has done nothing about the Redbrands
- Various leads about side quests and locations
- General information about Phandalin's residents and businesses

## What They Want

1. **Immediate:** Safety for his family
2. **Short-term:** See the Redbrands dealt with
3. **Long-term:** Phandalin becoming a prosperous, peaceful town

## The Stonehill Inn

The inn serves as:
- Primary lodging in Phandalin (5 sp/night for a room)
- Social gathering place for locals
- Source of local news and rumors
- Safe haven for travelers

**Staff:**
- Toblen Stonehill (proprietor)
- Trilena Stonehill (wife, helps run the inn)
- Pip Stonehill (son, sometimes helps out)
- Elsa (barmaid)

## Family

### Trilena Stonehill
- Toblen's wife
- Helps run the inn
- Knows about Thel Dendrar's death
- Later becomes a town councilor

### Pip Stonehill
- Their young son
- Has heard stories from Carp Alderleaf about the secret tunnel
- Eager and curious

## Reaction to Redbrands

Toblen is:
- Upset that the Redbrands have been terrorizing the town
- Frustrated that Townmaster Harbin Wester has done nothing
- Afraid to openly oppose them for fear of retaliation
- Grateful and relieved if the party deals with the threat
