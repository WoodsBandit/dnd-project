**Race/Ancestry:** Elf
**Occupation:** Priestess of Tymora
**Location:** Shrine of Luck, Phandalin
**Faction:** The Harpers
**Status:** Alive

An elven woman with the graceful bearing of her race. She maintains the humble attire appropriate to a small-town priestess while carrying herself with quiet confidence.

## Personality

- **Trait:** Kind and approachable; genuinely cares for the people of Phandalin
- **Ideal:** Freedom - Tymora teaches that luck favors the bold who seize opportunities
- **Bond:** Loyalty to the Harpers and their mission to oppose tyranny
- **Flaw:** Sometimes asks too much of those she recruits

## Voice/Mannerisms

Speaks with warmth and sincerity. Uses luck-based expressions ("Fortune smiles on the brave"). Becomes more businesslike when discussing Harper matters. Makes subtle references to observing events in town.

## Background

Sister Garaele serves as the priestess at the Shrine of Luck in Phandalin, ministering to followers of Tymora, goddess of luck. Beyond her religious duties, she is secretly an agent of the Harpers - a scattered network of adventurers and spies who advocate equality and covertly oppose the abuse of power.

She regularly reports to her Harper superiors on events in and around Phandalin.

## Relationship to Party

- **First Met:** Shrine of Luck in Phandalin
- **Disposition:** Friendly and helpful
- **Owes/Owed:** Will reward party for completing her quest

## What They Know

- Local gossip and information about Phandalin
- The location of Agatha the banshee near Conyberry
- Harper intelligence about regional threats
- That the Redbrands have been causing trouble
- Rumors about Thundertree and other locations

## What They Want

1. **Immediate:** Have someone question the banshee Agatha about a lost spellbook
2. **Short-term:** See the Redbrand threat neutralized
3. **Long-term:** Recruit capable agents for the Harpers

## Quest Given

### The Banshee's Bargain
- **Objective:** Travel to the ruins of Conyberry and seek out the banshee Agatha. Give her a silver comb as a gift, then ask her about the location of a spellbook belonging to the legendary mage Bowgentle.
- **Background:** Sister Garaele attempted this herself but was unable to complete the mission
- **Reward:** 3 potions of healing
- **Faction Opportunity:** Successful completion may lead to Harper recruitment

## The Harpers

Sister Garaele can share information about the Harpers:
- A network of spellcasters and spies dedicated to promoting good
- They gather information and oppose tyrants
- They work covertly to avoid direct confrontation
- Membership is by invitation only

**Harper Recruitment:**
If the party successfully completes her quest and shows alignment with Harper values, she may offer membership (rank of Watcher).

## The Shrine of Luck

The Shrine of Luck is a small temple dedicated to Tymora:
- Located on the east side of Phandalin
- Modest but well-maintained
- Provides healing and blessings to those in need
- Serves as Sister Garaele's home and base of operations
