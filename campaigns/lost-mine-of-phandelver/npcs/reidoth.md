**Race/Ancestry:** Human
**Occupation:** Druid
**Location:** Thundertree (currently)
**Faction:** Emerald Enclave
**Status:** Alive

## Appearance

An elderly human with the weathered look of someone who has spent a lifetime outdoors. Wears simple, practical clothing suited to wilderness travel. Has a wise, patient demeanor.

## Personality

- **Trait:** Patient and observant; speaks only when he has something worth saying
- **Ideal:** Balance - Nature must be preserved and protected
- **Bond:** The wilderness of the North, particularly this region
- **Flaw:** Sometimes too focused on natural concerns to address human problems

## Voice/Mannerisms

Speaks slowly and deliberately. Uses nature metaphors. Takes time to observe before acting. Quiet intensity when discussing threats to nature. Comfortable with long silences.

## Background

Reidoth is an elderly druid who has spent decades living in and protecting the wilderness of the North. He travels throughout the region, observing changes and addressing threats to the natural balance.

He is a member of the Emerald Enclave, an organization dedicated to preserving the natural order.

Recently, he traveled to Thundertree to investigate reports of undead and to assess the dragon that has taken up residence there.

## Relationship to Party

- **First Met:** Thundertree (at the old druid's cottage)
- **Disposition:** Neutral to friendly (depends on party's respect for nature)
- **Owes/Owed:** Will share information in exchange for help

## What They Know

- The layout of Thundertree
- The green dragon Venomfang's location
- The locations of undead in Thundertree
- The location of Cragmaw Castle
- Regional geography and wilderness paths
- The presence of cultists in Thundertree
- General knowledge of the Sword Coast's natural world

## What They Want

1. **Immediate:** Drive the dragon from Thundertree
2. **Short-term:** Clear the undead from the ruins
3. **Long-term:** See Thundertree's natural recovery

## The Thundertree Problem

Reidoth is dealing with multiple issues:
- **Venomfang:** A young green dragon has taken residence in the tower
- **Undead:** Zombies and ash zombies infest the ruins
- **Cultists:** Dragon cultists have arrived to worship Venomfang
- **Blights:** Twig blights lurk throughout the area

## Information About Cragmaw Castle

Reidoth knows:
- The location of Cragmaw Castle
- How to reach it through the wilderness
- That goblins inhabit the ruins
- Will share this information if the party helps with Thundertree's problems

## The Emerald Enclave

Reidoth represents the Emerald Enclave:
- Dedicated to preserving the natural order
- Opposes threats to the wilderness
- Works to maintain balance between civilization and nature
- Reidoth may recommend worthy party members for membership

## Quest: Drive Off Venomfang

Reidoth's primary concern:
- The dragon's presence threatens the region
- He cannot handle Venomfang alone
- He asks the party to help drive off or defeat the dragon
- Emphasizes that killing isn't necessary - just making it leave

## Guidance Through Thundertree

If the party helps him, Reidoth:
- Warns them about the various dangers
- Points out locations of undead
- Identifies the cultist presence
- Explains how to find the dragon's lair
