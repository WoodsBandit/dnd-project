# Qelline Alderleaf

**Race/Ancestry:** Halfling
**Occupation:** Farmer
**Location:** Alderleaf Farm, Phandalin
**Faction:** None (friend of the Emerald Enclave)
**Status:** Alive

## Appearance

A wise-looking female halfling of forty-five years. She has the practical appearance of a working farmer - weathered hands, simple but well-maintained clothing, and a knowing look in her eyes.

## Personality

- **Trait:** Pragmatic and observant; seems to know everything happening in town
- **Ideal:** Community - Neighbors help neighbors
- **Bond:** Her son Carp and the wellbeing of Phandalin
- **Flaw:** Sometimes too trusting of those she perceives as good

## Voice/Mannerisms

Speaks with measured wisdom. Takes her time before answering. Offers hospitality naturally. Watches people carefully. Uses farming metaphors. Maternal warmth when interacting with children or those who remind her of them.

## Background

Qelline has lived in Phandalin for many years, running a successful farm on the southeastern edge of town. She is a fixture of the community, known for her practical wisdom and her ability to keep track of local goings-on.

She maintains a friendship with Reidoth, a druid who sometimes passes through the area.

## Relationship to Party

- **First Met:** Alderleaf Farm in Phandalin
- **Disposition:** Friendly and welcoming
- **Owes/Owed:** Offers hospitality; grateful if the party helps protect the town

## What They Know

- Extensive knowledge of local gossip and events
- Her friend Reidoth the druid recently went to Thundertree
- Reidoth would know where Cragmaw Castle is
- The Redbrands have been causing trouble
- Her son Carp found a secret tunnel near Tresendar Manor

## What They Want

1. **Immediate:** Safety for her son Carp
2. **Short-term:** The Redbrand threat dealt with
3. **Long-term:** Peace and prosperity for Phandalin

## Key Information

### The Secret Tunnel
Qelline's son Carp discovered a secret tunnel while playing near Tresendar Manor:
- The tunnel is hidden in a thicket
- "Big ugly bandits" (bugbears) came out of it
- They met with Redbrands
- This provides an alternate entrance to the Redbrand Hideout

### Reidoth the Druid
Qelline knows Reidoth well:
- He's a druid who travels the region
- He recently went to Thundertree to investigate undead there
- He would know the location of Cragmaw Castle
- He can be found in Thundertree if the party seeks him

## Hospitality

Qelline offers:
- Lodging in her hayloft (free alternative to the inn)
- Meals and conversation
- Local knowledge and advice
- A friendly base of operations

## Family

### Carp Alderleaf
- Qelline's son, a halfling boy of about ten years
- Spirited and precocious
- Enchanted by the idea of adventurers
- Discovered the secret tunnel
- Can lead the party to the tunnel entrance if asked

## Alderleaf Farm

The farm:
- Located on the southeastern edge of Phandalin
- Produces vegetables and other crops
- Well-maintained and productive
- Has a barn with a hayloft for guests
- A comfortable, welcoming homestead

## Connection to Emerald Enclave

While not a member herself, Qelline's friendship with Reidoth connects her to the Emerald Enclave:
- She shares their values of living in harmony with nature
- She can point the party toward Reidoth
- Through Reidoth, the party may learn about the Enclave
