# Glasstaff (Iarno Albrek)

**Race/Ancestry:** Human
**Occupation:** Wizard, Criminal Leader, Former Lords' Alliance Agent
**Location:** Tresendar Manor (Redbrand Hideout)
**Faction:** Redbrands (leader), secretly serves the Black Spider; formerly Lords' Alliance
**Status:** Alive

## Appearance

A short, dark-bearded human wizard. He wears fine robes and carries his signature weapon - a staff of defense that appears to be made of glass, giving him his alias.

## Personality

- **Trait:** Intelligent and calculating; maintains an air of sophistication
- **Ideal:** Power - Arcane knowledge and wealth bring true power
- **Bond:** Self-interest above all; alliance with the Black Spider offers opportunity
- **Flaw:** Cowardly - will flee rather than face a losing battle; underestimates others

## Voice/Mannerisms

Speaks with educated precision. Condescending toward those he considers beneath him. Becomes nervous and defensive when cornered. May try to negotiate or deceive his way out of trouble.

## Background

Iarno Albrek was a wizard and member of the Lords' Alliance. He was sent to Phandalin to establish order in the frontier town. However, upon arriving and exploring Tresendar Manor, he saw an opportunity for personal gain.

Approximately two months before the adventure begins, Iarno:
- Explored Tresendar Manor and its cellars
- Made an arrangement with the nothic that laired there
- Established the Redbrands gang under the alias "Glasstaff"
- Made contact with Nezznar the Black Spider
- Accepted Nezznar's offer to share in the wealth of Wave Echo Cave in exchange for his loyalty

- **First Met:** Confrontation in Tresendar Manor

## What They Know

- The Black Spider is a drow
- The Black Spider is searching Wave Echo Cave for the Forge of Spells
- Three bugbears work for him (sent by the Black Spider)
- The bugbears know the way to Wave Echo Cave, but Iarno does not
- Sildar Hallwinter is looking for him

## What They Want

1. **Immediate:** Maintain control of Phandalin through fear and intimidation
2. **Short-term:** Assist the Black Spider in finding the Forge of Spells
3. **Long-term:** Share in the magical treasures of Wave Echo Cave

## The Redbrands

Iarno leads approximately a dozen Redbrand ruffians who:
- Terrorize the townspeople
- Shake down local businesses for protection money
- Killed Thel Dendrar (a woodcarver who stood up to them)
- Kidnapped Mirna Dendrar and her children
- Operate out of Tresendar Manor
