# Sildar Hallwinter

**Race/Ancestry:** Human
**Occupation:** Retired Knight, Lords' Alliance Agent
**Location:** Initially captive in Cragmaw Hideout; later at Townmaster's Hall in Phandalin
**Faction:** Lords' Alliance
**Status:** Alive (captive at adventure start)

## Appearance

A nearly 50-year-old human male with a dignified bearing despite his current circumstances. Carries himself like the soldier he once was. Distinguished features that speak to years of service.

## Personality

- **Trait:** Restrained and almost cold in mannerisms and speech, but sincere
- **Ideal:** Justice - The rule of law must be maintained to protect civilization
- **Bond:** Loyalty to the Lords' Alliance and his old friend Iarno Albrek
- **Flaw:** Lacking in confidence and charisma; can be risk-averse (though willing to act when no one else will)

## Voice/Mannerisms

Speak in a formal, measured way. Professional military bearing. Sincere but not warm. Becomes more animated when discussing matters of law and order. Uses formal titles and shows respect for proper authority.

## Background

In his younger days, Sildar served with distinction in the Griffon Cavalry of Waterdeep. He earned a place of honor through his military service. Now semi-retired, he works as an agent for the Lords' Alliance, helping to maintain stability in the North.

Sildar met Gundren Rockseeker in Neverwinter and agreed to accompany him to Phandalin. He also has a personal mission: to investigate the disappearance of Iarno Albrek, a fellow Lords' Alliance member who vanished shortly after arriving in Phandalin.

## Relationship to Party

- **First Met:** Rescued from Cragmaw Hideout
- **Disposition:** Grateful and friendly (if rescued)
- **Owes/Owed:** Owes the party his life; will pay 50 gp for his rescue

## What They Know

- Gundren was taken to "somewhere called Cragmaw Castle" by the goblin leader
- Iarno Albrek was sent to Phandalin by the Lords' Alliance to establish order
- The Lords' Alliance has an interest in stabilizing Phandalin
- General information about the political situation in the North
- **Does NOT know:** That Iarno has become Glasstaff and leads the Redbrands

## What They Want

1. **Immediate:** To be rescued and reach Phandalin safely
2. **Short-term:** Find Iarno Albrek (his friend and fellow agent)
3. **Medium-term:** Rescue Gundren Rockseeker from Cragmaw Castle
4. **Long-term:** Establish law and order in Phandalin; see the town prosper

## Quests Given

### Find Iarno Albrek
- **Objective:** Discover what happened to the missing Lords' Alliance agent
- **Reward:** 200 gp
- **Faction:** Lords' Alliance membership opportunity

### Rescue Gundren Rockseeker
- **Objective:** Find and rescue Gundren from Cragmaw Castle
- **Reward:** 500 gp

## After Rescue

Once rescued and in Phandalin, Sildar:
- Establishes himself at the Townmaster's Hall
- Offers quests to find Iarno and rescue Gundren
- Becomes a town councilor
- Can provide support and information throughout the adventure
- Works alongside (sometimes uneasily with) other councilors including Harbin Wester and Halia Thornton

## Reaction to Learning About Glasstaff

When the party reveals that Iarno Albrek is Glasstaff, Sildar is:
- Shocked and devastated by his friend's betrayal
- Determined to see Iarno brought to justice
- If Iarno is captured alive, Sildar wants him taken to Neverwinter for trial
