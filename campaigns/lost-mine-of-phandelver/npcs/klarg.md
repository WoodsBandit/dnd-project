# Klarg

**Race/Ancestry:** Bugbear
**Occupation:** Raid Leader, Commander of Cragmaw Hideout
**Location:** Cragmaw Hideout
**Faction:** Cragmaw Tribe (serves King Grol)
**Status:** Alive

## Appearance

A large, brutish bugbear with the arrogance of a would-be warlord. Wears trophies from his raids and carries himself with inflated self-importance.

## Personality

- **Trait:** Filled with delusions of grandeur; sees himself as a mighty warlord
- **Ideal:** Conquest - He is destined for greatness
- **Bond:** His pet wolf Ripper
- **Flaw:** Overestimates his own importance; can be negotiated with if his ego is stroked

## Voice/Mannerisms

Speaks in third person ("Klarg is great!"). Boasts constantly about his accomplishments. Prone to dramatic declarations. Easily flattered. Becomes enraged when his authority is questioned.

## Background

Klarg leads the Cragmaw goblins stationed at the hideout along the Triboar Trail. He has orders from King Grol to ambush and plunder poorly defended caravans and travelers, and received special instructions regarding Gundren Rockseeker.

He received a messenger goblin from King Grol informing him that "someone named the Black Spider" was paying the Cragmaws to capture Gundren and anything he was carrying.

1. **Immediate:** Defend his lair and prove his worth

3. **Long-term:** Become a great warlord (in his own mind)

## The Ambush

Klarg organized the ambush on Gundren and Sildar:
- Goblins attacked the travelers on the Triboar Trail
- Gundren was captured and sent to Cragmaw Castle
- Sildar was kept at the hideout
- Gundren's belongings were sent to King Grol
- The horses were killed and left as bait

## Cragmaw Hideout Forces

Klarg commands:
- Two goblin assistants in his cave
- Ripper, his pet wolf
- Various goblins throughout the hideout
- Sildar Hallwinter is held prisoner in another chamber
