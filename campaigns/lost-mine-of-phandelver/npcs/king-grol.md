# King Grol

**Race/Ancestry:** Bugbear
**Occupation:** Chieftain of the Cragmaw Tribe
**Location:** Cragmaw Castle
**Faction:** Cragmaw Tribe (serves the Black Spider)
**Status:** Alive

## Appearance

An old, fierce bugbear whose age has stooped his shoulders and hunched his back. Despite this, he remains surprisingly agile and strong. His appearance is battle-worn and intimidating.

## Personality

- **Trait:** Demanding and vindictive; rules through pure intimidation
- **Ideal:** Power - Strength is the only thing that matters
- **Bond:** His authority over the Cragmaw tribe
- **Flaw:** Cruel and short-tempered; no Cragmaw dares cross him

## Voice/Mannerisms

Speaks in a gruff, commanding growl. Issues orders, doesn't ask. Quick to violence when displeased. Suspicious of everyone, even his own subordinates.

## Background

King Grol has ruled the Cragmaw tribe for many years through sheer intimidation and brutality. When the Black Spider offered payment to work for him, Grol saw an opportunity for wealth and agreed to the arrangement.

Under Grol's leadership, the Cragmaws have been:
- Ambushing travelers on the Triboar Trail
- Capturing prisoners for the Black Spider
- Raiding and plundering the surrounding area

## The Situation at Cragmaw Castle

When the party arrives:
- Grol has Gundren Rockseeker as a prisoner
- He possesses the map to Wave Echo Cave
- A doppelganger (disguised as a female drow) is present
- The doppelganger represents the Black Spider's interests
- Negotiations are ongoing for the prisoner and map
