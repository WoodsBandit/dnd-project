## Personality

- **Trait:** Principled and direct; doesn't mince words
- **Ideal:** Justice - Evil must be opposed wherever it's found
- **Bond:** Loyalty to the Order of the Gauntlet and its mission
- **Flaw:** Can be judgmental of those who don't take action against evil

## Voice/Mannerisms

Speaks with the confidence of experience. Direct eye contact. Gives unsolicited advice. References his adventuring days when relevant. Firm handshake. Assessing look when meeting new people.

## Background

Daran Edermath is a retired adventurer who fought many battles against orcs, undead, and other threats during his active years. He served as a marshal and herald for many years, standing as a champion of justice.

Now in his twilight years, he has settled in Phandalin to run an apple orchard. However, he maintains his membership in the Order of the Gauntlet and remains vigilant against evil.

## Relationship to Party

- **First Met:** Edermath Orchard in Phandalin
- **Disposition:** Friendly to those who seem heroic; suspicious of moral ambiguity
- **Owes/Owed:** Respect and gratitude for dealing with threats

## What They Know

- Extensive knowledge of combat tactics and monster behavior
- Stories of his adventuring days
- The Redbrands are a stain on the town
- Undead have been sighted near Old Owl Well
- The Order of the Gauntlet's mission and values

## What They Want

1. **Immediate:** Someone to investigate the undead at Old Owl Well
2. **Short-term:** The Redbrand threat eliminated
3. **Long-term:** Righteous heroes to carry on the fight against evil

## Quest Given

### Old Owl Well
- **Objective:** Find out who is behind the undead sightings at Old Owl Well
- **Background:** Prospectors have reported undead activity near the old watchtower ruins
- **Reward:** Daran's respect and friendship (no monetary reward)
- **Faction Opportunity:** May lead to Order of the Gauntlet membership

**What the Party Will Find:** A Red Wizard of Thay named Hamun Kost has set up camp at Old Owl Well, using zombie minions to guard his archaeological work.

## The Order of the Gauntlet

Daran can share information about the Order:
- A devoted group of paladins, clerics, and other faithful
- They seek to protect the innocent and destroy evil
- Members are expected to be righteous and actively oppose darkness
- Membership requires demonstrated virtue

**Order Recruitment:**
If the party shows heroic qualities and successfully deals with threats to the region, Daran may recommend them for membership (rank of Chevall).
