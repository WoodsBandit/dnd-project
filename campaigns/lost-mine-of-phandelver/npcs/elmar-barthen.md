# Elmar Barthen

**Race/Ancestry:** Human
**Occupation:** Shopkeeper
**Location:** Barthen's Provisions, Phandalin
**Faction:** None (merchant)
**Status:** Alive

## Appearance

A lean, balding human male of about fifty years with a kindly manner. He has the patient, welcoming demeanor of an experienced shopkeeper.

## Personality

- **Trait:** Kindly and helpful; genuinely cares about customers
- **Ideal:** Service - A good shopkeeper serves the community
- **Bond:** His business and his connection to Gundren Rockseeker
- **Flaw:** Perhaps too trusting; doesn't like to think badly of people

## Voice/Mannerisms

Speaks with warmth and patience. Takes time to help customers find what they need. Expresses genuine concern when discussing friends in trouble. Professional but not cold.

## Background

Elmar Barthen runs Barthen's Provisions, the largest trading post in Phandalin. He has built a successful business serving the needs of prospectors, settlers, and travelers passing through.

He is a friend of Gundren Rockseeker and was expecting the delivery of mining supplies that the party was hired to escort.

## Relationship to Party

- **First Met:** Barthen's Provisions (delivering supplies)
- **Disposition:** Friendly (especially if party mentions Gundren)
- **Owes/Owed:** Pays the party for the delivery (10 gp each)

## What They Know

- Gundren Rockseeker's plans and whereabouts (to a degree)
- That Gundren's brothers Nundro and Tharden are camped outside town
- Local prices and available goods
- General news about Phandalin
- That something has happened to Gundren (he should have arrived by now)

## What They Want

1. **Immediate:** To receive the supplies he ordered
2. **Short-term:** To learn what happened to Gundren
3. **Long-term:** A prosperous business in a peaceful town

## The Delivery

When the party arrives with the wagon:
- Barthen pays them 10 gp each as promised
- He expresses concern that Gundren hasn't arrived
- He mentions Gundren's brothers should be coming to town soon
- He can provide information about the town

## Barthen's Provisions

The trading post:
- Largest trading post in Phandalin
- Open from sunup to sundown
- Stocks most ordinary goods and supplies:
  - Backpacks, bedrolls, rope, rations
  - Standard adventuring equipment
  - Items up to 25 gp in value
- Does NOT stock weapons or armor
- Employs two young clerks: Ander and Thistle

**Pricing:** Standard PHB prices for available goods

## Staff

### Ander and Thistle
- Young clerks who work at the store
- Can help customers when Barthen is busy
- Know less about local events than Barthen does

## Information from Barthen

Barthen can share:
- Concern about Gundren's delayed arrival
- That the Rockseeker brothers found "something big"
- The brothers have been secretive about their discovery
- Suggestions about where to stay and who to talk to in town
- General warnings about the Redbrands
