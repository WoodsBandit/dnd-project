# Gundren Rockseeker

**Race/Ancestry:** Dwarf
**Occupation:** Prospector, Treasure Hunter
**Location:** Captured - initially Cragmaw Hideout, then Cragmaw Castle
**Faction:** Allied with Lords' Alliance
**Status:** Alive (captive)

## Appearance

A stout dwarf with a thick beard and the weathered look of someone who has spent years prospecting in the wilderness. His eyes gleam with excitement when discussing treasure or mining prospects.

## Personality

- **Trait:** Excitable and impatient, especially regarding the discovery of Wave Echo Cave
- **Ideal:** Prosperity - Reclaiming the lost mine will bring wealth and renown to his family
- **Bond:** Deep loyalty to his brothers Tharden and Nundro; friendship with Sildar Hallwinter
- **Flaw:** His excitement and impatience led him to ride ahead, making himself vulnerable to ambush

## Voice/Mannerisms

Speak with enthusiasm that borders on giddiness when discussing the mine. Uses mining terminology. Becomes secretive when asked about specifics of the discovery. Classic dwarven accent with a businesslike manner.

## Background

Gundren is one of three Rockseeker brothers (the others being Tharden and Nundro). Together, they spent considerable time and effort searching for the legendary Wave Echo Cave. When they finally discovered the entrance, Gundren excitedly rushed to Neverwinter to gather supplies and hire adventurers to escort them to Phandalin.

## Relationship to Party

- **First Met:** Before the adventure begins (hired the party in Neverwinter)
- **Disposition:** Friendly (employer)
- **Owes/Owed:** Owes the party 10 gp each for the escort mission; will owe much more for his rescue

## What They Know

- The location of Wave Echo Cave (has the map)
- The history of the Phandelver's Pact and the Forge of Spells
- His brothers are camped somewhere outside Phandalin
- General layout of the Phandalin area

## What They Want

1. **Immediate:** To be rescued from captivity
2. **Short-term:** To reunite with his brothers and reclaim Wave Echo Cave
3. **Long-term:** To restore the Forge of Spells and bring prosperity to the region

## Current Situation

Gundren was ambushed on the Triboar Trail by Cragmaw goblins working for the Black Spider. He was initially taken to Cragmaw Hideout, then transferred to Cragmaw Castle and held by King Grol. The map to Wave Echo Cave was taken from him.

His brother Tharden has been killed by Nezznar. His brother Nundro is also a captive in Wave Echo Cave.
