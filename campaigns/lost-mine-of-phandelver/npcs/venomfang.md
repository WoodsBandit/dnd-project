# Venomfang

**Race/Ancestry:** Green Dragon (Young)
**Occupation:** Predator, Territorial Monster
**Location:** Thundertree (ruined tower)
**Faction:** None (served by dragon cultists)
**Status:** Alive

## Appearance

A young green dragon with emerald scales and a cruel, intelligent gleam in its eyes. Despite its youth, it is still a formidable and terrifying creature.

## Personality

- **Trait:** Cunning and deceptive; enjoys tormenting prey
- **Ideal:** Dominance - All lesser creatures should bow to dragons
- **Bond:** Territorial pride in its new lair
- **Flaw:** Arrogant; underestimates non-dragons but is pragmatic about survival

## Voice/Mannerisms

Speaks with cold, predatory intelligence. Takes pleasure in others' fear. Uses flattery and manipulation. Switches between charming and threatening. Always calculating advantage.

## Background

Venomfang is a young green dragon that has recently claimed the ruined tower in Thundertree as its lair. Its arrival has driven away or killed many of the area's inhabitants and attracted the attention of dragon cultists.

The dragon's presence concerns Reidoth the druid, who knows the creature will eventually expand its territory and become a greater threat.

## Relationship to Party

- **First Met:** Thundertree (ruined tower)
- **Disposition:** Hostile (predator)
- **Owes/Owed:** N/A

## What They Know

- The layout of Thundertree
- The cultists worship it (and are useful idiots)
- Regional geography from aerial observation
- That humanoids are weak but sometimes dangerous in groups

## What They Want

1. **Immediate:** Defend its new territory; acquire treasure
2. **Short-term:** Establish a proper hoard
3. **Long-term:** Grow in power and expand territory

## The Encounter

Venomfang presents a dangerous challenge:

**Combat Option:**
- Venomfang is a deadly opponent for low-level parties
- The original adventure suggests it flees at 50% HP
- A straight fight is very risky at level 3-4

**Negotiation Option:**
- Venomfang may parley if approached carefully
- Could provide information in exchange for treasure or service
- Will betray any agreement when convenient
- Might be convinced to leave (temporarily) for the right price

**Avoidance Option:**
- The party can complete other objectives without fighting
- The tower can be scouted from a distance
- Reidoth may warn them about the dragon's power

## The Dragon Cultists

A group of dragon cultists has arrived in Thundertree:
- Led by a human named Favric
- They hope to recruit Venomfang to their cause
- Venomfang finds them useful but doesn't truly ally with them
- The cultists may complicate encounters

## Lair

The ruined tower:
- Tallest structure in Thundertree
- Good vantage point for hunting
- Contains the beginnings of a treasure hoard
- Dangerous approach (dragon can see visitors coming)

## Warning Signs

Reidoth warns the party:
- The dragon is extremely dangerous
- Even young dragons are deadly
- Discretion is the better part of valor
- Driving it off is as good as killing it
