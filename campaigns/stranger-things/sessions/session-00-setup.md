### 4. The Stranger Things Connection
Explain to players:
- The adventure is "written" by Mike Wheeler from the show
- The Upside Down and Demogorgon appear (D&D versions)
- The Thessalhydra is referenced in Season 1
- Nostalgia and 1980s aesthetic are intentional

### 5. Tone Expectations
- Heroic adventure with horror elements
- Combat-forward but with roleplay opportunities
- Classic D&D tropes embraced
- Fun and accessible

## Character Introductions

### Option A: Quick Start
- Hand out character sheets
- Brief overview of each character's abilities
- Jump straight into the adventure

### Option B: Extended Introduction
- Players read their full character sheets
- Practice a combat roll or two
- Do a quick "how did you all meet?" scene
- Then begin the adventure

## Rules Overview (for New Players)

If players are new to D&D, cover:
1. The basic d20 mechanic (roll + modifier vs. target)
2. Combat basics (attack, damage, AC, HP)
3. Each character's main abilities
4. When to roleplay vs. when to roll
5. How to read the character sheet

The Starter Set Rulebook covers levels 1-5 and essential rules.
