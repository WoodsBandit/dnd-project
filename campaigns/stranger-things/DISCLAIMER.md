This campaign is **fan-created content inspired by** the official D&D Stranger Things Starter Set (2019) published by Wizards of the Coast.

**This is NOT a reproduction of the official adventure.**

## What Is Verified

The following elements are confirmed to exist in the official Starter Set based on public sources:

- "Hunt for the Thessalhydra" adventure title
- 5 pre-generated Level 3 characters (Half-Elf Wizard, Human Paladin, Wood Elf Cleric, Half-Orc Ranger, Hill Dwarf Bard)
- Sir Tristan as quest giver in Crabapple Valley
- 100 gp per Thessalhydra head reward structure
- Troglodyte Caverns (with negotiation possible via leader who speaks Common)
- Cursed Labyrinth with random path mechanics
- The Lost Knight presenting riddles
- The Upside Down section
- Demogorgon encounter (can be wounded and escaped)
- Thessalhydra as final boss (8 heads, fire stops regeneration)
- Winter's Dark Bite scaling greatsword
