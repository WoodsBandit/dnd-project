# Will Byers - Will the Wise (Meta-Character)

**Role:** Player in Mike's D&D group
**Show:** Stranger Things (Netflix)
**Character Inspiration:** Cleric class ("Will the Wise")

## Overview

Will Byers is the heart of Stranger Things' central mystery and a core member of "The Party." His D&D character, "Will the Wise," is explicitly referenced in the show.

## Character in the Show

### Personality Traits
- Sensitive and artistic
- Deeply loyal to friends
- Creative and imaginative
- Resilient despite trauma
- Connected to the supernatural

### Notable Characteristics
- Central to Season 1's disappearance mystery
- Has lasting connection to the Upside Down
- Artistic talents (drawing)
- Quiet but observant

## D&D Connection

Will's character "Will the Wise" is the most explicitly named D&D persona from the show:
- **Class:** Cleric (confirmed as "Will the Wise")
- **Play Style:** Supportive, wise beyond years, perceptive
- **Roleplay:** The spiritual heart of the party

The Starter Set includes a **Wood Elf Cleric** pre-generated character that directly connects to Will the Wise.

## Famous D&D Moments from the Show

- "Will the Wise" is referenced multiple times
- His disappearance parallels being lost in a dungeon dimension
- Returns to D&D as therapy and connection after trauma
- The Thessalhydra finale roll is his character's moment

## The Upside Down Connection

Will's real-world experience in the Upside Down parallels a D&D adventure gone horribly real - trapped in another dimension, hunted by monsters, needing rescue by friends.

## Notes

Will represents the player who brings emotional depth and wisdom to the game, often seeing things others miss. His character name "Will the Wise" perfectly encapsulates the Cleric role.
