# Dustin Henderson (Meta-Character)

**Role:** Player in Mike's D&D group
**Show:** Stranger Things (Netflix)
**Character Inspiration:** Bard class

## Overview

Dustin Henderson is a member of "The Party" and one of the main characters in Stranger Things. His enthusiasm, intelligence, and heart make him a beloved character both in the show and at the D&D table.

## Character in the Show

### Personality Traits
- Highly intelligent and curious
- Optimistic and enthusiastic
- Loyal friend
- Pop culture savvy
- Scientific mindset

### Notable Characteristics
- Known for his humor and one-liners
- Often provides comic relief
- Has a unique perspective on problems
- Values knowledge and understanding

## D&D Connection

Dustin's character in Mike's games likely reflects his personality:
- **Class Inspiration:** Bard (charisma, knowledge, versatility)
- **Play Style:** Creative problem-solver, supportive teammate
- **Roleplay:** Likely brings humor and enthusiasm to sessions

The Starter Set includes a **Hill Dwarf Bard** pre-generated character that may evoke Dustin's energy.
