# NPCs - Hunt for the Thessalhydra

This folder contains NPC files for the Stranger Things D&D Starter Set adventure.

## Key NPCs

### Quest Givers
- `sir-tristan.md` - The noble who sends the party on their quest

### Allies / Neutral
- `the-lost-knight.md` - Guardian of the passage to the Upside Down
- `road-npcs.md` - The four NPCs encountered during "Rumors on the Road"

### Antagonists
- `demogorgon.md` - The D&D demon encountered in the Upside Down
- `thessalhydra.md` - The final boss and target of the hunt
- `troglodytes.md` - The cavern-dwelling creatures (can be negotiated with)

## Meta: The Party Kids

The adventure is presented as Mike Wheeler's campaign from the show. Files for the kids as meta-characters:
- `meta-mike-wheeler.md` - The Dungeon Master
- `meta-dustin-henderson.md` - Player / Bard inspiration
- `meta-lucas-sinclair.md` - Player / Ranger inspiration
- `meta-will-byers.md` - Player / Cleric inspiration (Will the Wise)
- `meta-eleven.md` - The real-world hero with psychic powers

## Organization

NPCs are organized by their role in the adventure. Each file follows the campaign template format.
