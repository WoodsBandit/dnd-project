# Mike Wheeler (Meta-Character)

**Role:** Dungeon Master
**Show:** Stranger Things (Netflix)
**Status:** Campaign Creator

## Overview

Mike Wheeler is the author of the "Hunt for the Thessalhydra" adventure. The entire Starter Set adventure book is presented as his personal campaign journal, complete with handwritten notes and creative flourishes.

## Character in the Show

Mike is the de facto leader of "The Party" - the group of friends from Hawkins Middle School. He serves as the Dungeon Master for their D&D games, which becomes a thematic framework for understanding the supernatural events in their town.

### Personality Traits
- Natural leader and storyteller
- Protective of his friends
- Creative and imaginative
- Emotionally intense

### D&D Style as DM
- Creates engaging narrative adventures
- Balances combat with roleplay
- Incorporates classic D&D tropes
- Values player creativity

## Connection to This Adventure

The Stranger Things Starter Set presents the adventure as Mike's creation:
- The adventure book is styled as Mike's journal
- Handwritten notes appear throughout
- The 1980s aesthetic reflects Mike's era
- The Thessalhydra reference ties to Season 1's finale

## The Party (Mike's D&D Group)

Mike runs games for:
- Dustin Henderson
- Lucas Sinclair
- Will Byers
- (Later) Max Mayfield
- (Briefly/Spiritually) Eleven

## Famous Campaign Moment

In Season 1's finale, the kids finish a D&D session where Will rolls to attack the Thessalhydra. The roll's result is left ambiguous, mirroring the uncertainty of their real-world situation.

## Notes for DMs

When running this adventure, you can:
- Adopt Mike's narrative voice for flavor
- Reference the handwritten journal style
- Acknowledge the meta-nature of playing "Mike's campaign"
- Draw parallels to Stranger Things events if desired
