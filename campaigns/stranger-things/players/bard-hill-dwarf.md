# Hill Dwarf Bard

**Player:** [TBD]
**Race:** Hill Dwarf
**Class/Subclass:** Bard (College of Lore at Level 3)
**Level:** 3 (can advance to Level 5)
**Background:** Entertainer

## Stranger Things Connection

This character represents **Dustin Henderson** from the show. Dustin's charisma, enthusiasm, wit, and supportive nature align perfectly with the Bard class. He's the heart of the group, always ready with a quip or encouragement, and his encyclopedic knowledge of monsters and games translates into the Bard's Jack of All Trades.

## Appearance

A stout hill dwarf with a talent for music, performance, and inspiration. Hill dwarves are known for their toughness, wisdom, and stubborn resilience.

## Backstory Summary

The pre-generated characters focus on class, race, and background descriptions rather than extensive individual backstories. As an Entertainer, this bard has honed performance skills and learned to inspire others.

## Personality

- **Trait:** Enthusiastic and encouraging, always has something to say
- **Ideal:** Friendship - The bonds of the group are what matter most
- **Bond:** Knowledge is power; loves learning new things
- **Flaw:** Talks too much, sometimes at the wrong times

## Racial Traits (Hill Dwarf)

- **Ability Score Increase:** +2 Constitution, +1 Wisdom
- **Darkvision:** 60 feet
- **Dwarven Resilience:** Advantage on saves vs. poison, resistance to poison damage
- **Dwarven Combat Training:** Proficiency with battleaxe, handaxe, light hammer, warhammer
- **Stonecunning:** Double proficiency on History checks for stonework
- **Dwarven Toughness:** +1 HP per level (already factored into HP)
- **Speed:** 25 feet (not reduced by heavy armor)

## Class Features (Bard Level 3)

- **Bardic Inspiration:** d6 dice, CHA mod uses per long rest
- **Jack of All Trades:** Add half proficiency to non-proficient checks
- **Song of Rest:** Extra 1d6 healing during short rests
- **Spellcasting:** Charisma-based spellcasting
- **Expertise:** Double proficiency in two skills
- **Bard College - Lore:**
  - **Bonus Proficiencies:** 3 additional skill proficiencies
  - **Cutting Words:** Use Bardic Inspiration to reduce enemy rolls

## Role in the Party

The Bard serves as:
- **Support:** Bardic Inspiration, Cutting Words, buffs
- **Face:** High Charisma for social encounters
- **Healer:** Healing Word as backup
- **Utility:** Jack of All Trades makes them good at everything

## Combat Tactics

1. **Bardic Inspiration:** Give d6 to key allies before important rolls
2. **Cutting Words:** Reduce enemy attack/damage/ability checks as reaction
3. **Faerie Fire:** Grant advantage on attacks against multiple enemies
4. **Vicious Mockery:** Impose disadvantage on enemy attacks
5. **Healing Word:** Bonus action to revive downed allies

### Cutting Words (Lore Feature)

As a reaction when an enemy within 60 ft makes an attack, ability check, or damage roll:
- Expend one Bardic Inspiration die
- Subtract the roll from the enemy's result
- Must decide before knowing if it succeeds

## Jack of All Trades

Add +1 (half of +2 proficiency) to ALL ability checks you're not proficient in. This includes:
- Initiative rolls
- Counterspell/Dispel Magic ability checks
- Any skill check not already proficient
