# Wood Elf Cleric

**Player:** [TBD]
**Race:** Wood Elf
**Class/Subclass:** Cleric (Life Domain at Level 3)
**Level:** 3 (can advance to Level 5)
**Background:** Acolyte

## Stranger Things Connection

This character represents **Will Byers** from the show. Will's connection to the Upside Down and his sensitivity to supernatural forces translates perfectly into divine magic. As a Cleric, this character channels otherworldly power and serves as the party's spiritual anchor - just as Will's experiences shape the group's understanding of the supernatural threats they face. Will's D&D character was famously called "Will the Wise."

## Appearance

A graceful wood elf devoted to divine service. Wood elves are known for their connection to nature, swift movement, and keen senses.

## Backstory Summary

The pre-generated characters focus on class, race, and background descriptions rather than extensive individual backstories. As an Acolyte, this cleric has spent time in service to the divine, learning prayers and rituals.

## Personality

- **Trait:** Compassionate and perceptive, notices what others miss
- **Ideal:** Charity - Help those in need, regardless of cost
- **Bond:** Faith in something greater provides strength in darkness
- **Flaw:** Sometimes too trusting or hesitant to act

## Racial Traits (Wood Elf)

- **Ability Score Increase:** +2 Dexterity, +1 Wisdom
- **Darkvision:** 60 feet
- **Keen Senses:** Proficiency in Perception
- **Fey Ancestry:** Advantage on saves vs. charm, immunity to magical sleep
- **Trance:** 4 hours of meditation instead of 8 hours sleep
- **Elf Weapon Training:** Proficiency with longsword, shortsword, shortbow, longbow
- **Fleet of Foot:** Base speed 35 feet
- **Mask of the Wild:** Can hide in light natural phenomena

## Class Features (Cleric Level 3)

- **Spellcasting:** Wisdom-based divine casting
- **Divine Domain - Life:**
  - **Bonus Proficiency:** Heavy Armor
  - **Disciple of Life:** Healing spells heal extra 2 + spell level HP
  - **Channel Divinity:** Preserve Life (distribute 15 HP among allies within 30 ft)
- **Channel Divinity (1/short rest):** Turn Undead or Preserve Life

## Spell Slots (Level 3)

## Role in the Party

The Cleric serves as:
- **Healer:** Primary healing via Cure Wounds, Healing Word, Preserve Life
- **Support:** Bless, Shield of Faith, buffs
- **Secondary Combatant:** Spiritual Weapon, Sacred Flame
- **Utility:** Lesser Restoration, ritual casting

## Combat Tactics

1. **Opening:** Cast Bless on 3 allies (concentration)
2. **Bonus Action:** Spiritual Weapon for extra damage
3. **Healing Priority:** Healing Word to revive downed allies
4. **Preserve Life:** Channel Divinity when multiple allies hurt
