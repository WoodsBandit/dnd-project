# Human Paladin

**Player:** [TBD]
**Race:** Human
**Class/Subclass:** Paladin (Oath of Devotion at Level 3)
**Level:** 3 (can advance to Level 5)
**Background:** Noble or Soldier

## Stranger Things Connection

This character represents **Mike Wheeler** from the show. As the Dungeon Master of his friend group, Mike is the natural leader - and the Human Paladin embodies that leadership role. Paladins are protectors, strategists, and moral centers of their parties, just as Mike guides his friends through their adventures.

## Appearance

A stalwart human warrior clad in heavy armor, bearing the symbols of their sacred oath. Humans are versatile and determined, often rising to leadership through sheer willpower and dedication.

## Backstory Summary

The pre-generated characters focus on class, race, and background descriptions rather than extensive individual backstories. As a Paladin, this character has sworn a sacred oath and draws divine power from their devotion to justice and protection.

## Personality

- **Trait:** Protective of allies, takes charge in dangerous situations
- **Ideal:** Greater Good - Protecting the innocent is worth any sacrifice
- **Bond:** The party is family; their safety comes first
- **Flaw:** Can be stubborn about doing things "the right way"

## Racial Traits (Human)

- **Ability Score Increase:** +1 to all ability scores
- **Extra Language:** One additional language
- **Versatile:** Humans are adaptable and varied

## Class Features (Paladin Level 3)

- **Divine Sense:** Detect celestials, fiends, and undead (1 + CHA mod per long rest)
- **Lay on Hands:** Healing pool (Level x 5 = 15 HP)
- **Fighting Style:** Defense (+1 AC) or Great Weapon Fighting
- **Spellcasting:** Charisma-based divine spellcasting
- **Divine Smite:** Extra radiant damage on weapon hits (2d8+ per spell slot)
- **Divine Health:** Immunity to disease
- **Sacred Oath - Devotion:**
  - **Channel Divinity:** Sacred Weapon or Turn the Unholy
  - **Oath Spells:** Protection from Evil and Good, Sanctuary

## Spell Slots (Level 3)

## Combat Stats (Estimated)

- **Armor Class:** 18 (Chain Mail + Shield) or 19 with Ring
- **Hit Points:** ~28 (10 + 2d10 + CON modifier x 3)
- **Speed:** 30 feet
- **Proficiency Bonus:** +2

## Role in the Party

The Paladin serves as:
- **Tank:** High AC, good HP, frontline fighter
- **Damage Dealer:** Divine Smite for burst damage
- **Off-Healer:** Lay on Hands for emergency healing
- **Leader:** High Charisma for social encounters

## Combat Tactics

1. **Frontline:** Stand between enemies and squishier allies
2. **Smite Decision:** Save slots for crits or important enemies
3. **Lay on Hands:** Emergency healing, especially for downed allies
4. **Greatsword:** Use Sir Tristan's magical weapon against the Thessalhydra
