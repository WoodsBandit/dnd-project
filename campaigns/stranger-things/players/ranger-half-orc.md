# Half-Orc Ranger

**Player:** [TBD]
**Race:** Half-Orc
**Class/Subclass:** Ranger (Hunter Conclave at Level 3)
**Level:** 3 (can advance to Level 5)
**Background:** Outlander

## Stranger Things Connection

This character represents **Lucas Sinclair** from the show. Lucas's practical, prepared nature and his signature wrist-rocket translate perfectly into the Ranger class. He's tactical, cautious, and always ready for danger - the perfect scout and ranged combatant.

## Appearance

A rugged half-orc skilled in tracking and wilderness survival. Half-orcs combine orcish strength with human adaptability, often serving as scouts and hunters.

## Backstory Summary

The pre-generated characters focus on class, race, and background descriptions rather than extensive individual backstories. As an Outlander, this ranger grew up in the wild, learning to track prey and survive in harsh conditions.

## Personality

- **Trait:** Practical and prepared, always has a plan
- **Ideal:** Self-Reliance - Trust your own skills and instincts
- **Bond:** Loyal to friends who prove themselves worthy
- **Flaw:** Can be skeptical or slow to trust

## Racial Traits (Half-Orc)

- **Ability Score Increase:** +2 Strength, +1 Constitution
- **Darkvision:** 60 feet
- **Menacing:** Proficiency in Intimidation
- **Relentless Endurance:** Drop to 1 HP instead of 0 (once per long rest)
- **Savage Attacks:** Extra damage die on critical hits with melee weapons

## Class Features (Ranger Level 3)

- **Favored Enemy:** Likely Monstrosities (for the Thessalhydra hunt)
- **Natural Explorer:** Appropriate terrain for the adventure
- **Fighting Style:** Archery (+2 to ranged attacks) or Two-Weapon Fighting
- **Spellcasting:** Wisdom-based nature magic
- **Ranger Archetype - Hunter:**
  - **Hunter's Prey:** Colossus Slayer (extra 1d8 on wounded targets)
- **Primeval Awareness:** Sense specific creature types

## Spell Slots (Level 3)
- 1st Level: 3 slots

## Spells Known (3 spells)

- Hunter's Mark (bonus damage, tracking)
- Cure Wounds (self-healing)
- Ensnaring Strike or Hail of Thorns

## Equipment

## Combat Stats (Estimated)

- **Armor Class:** 15-16 (Scale Mail) or higher with Ring
- **Hit Points:** ~28 (10 + 2d10 + CON modifier x 3)
- **Speed:** 30 feet
- **Proficiency Bonus:** +2

## Role in the Party

The Ranger serves as:
- **Ranged Damage:** Longbow + Hunter's Mark + Colossus Slayer
- **Scout:** Tracking, Perception, Stealth
- **Striker:** Effective in melee with two-weapon fighting
- **Utility:** Nature spells and survival skills

## Combat Tactics

1. **Opening:** Cast Hunter's Mark on the biggest threat
2. **Positioning:** Stay at range, use cover
3. **Colossus Slayer:** Extra 1d8 damage per turn on wounded targets
4. **Relentless Endurance:** Safety net if dropped to 0 HP

### Damage Output (Level 3)

| Attack | Damage |
|--------|--------|
| Longbow | 1d8 + DEX |
| + Hunter's Mark | + 1d6 |
| + Colossus Slayer | + 1d8 (if target wounded) |
| **Total** | Up to 1d8 + 1d6 + 1d8 + DEX |

## Leveling Notes

**Level 4:** Ability Score Improvement (+2 DEX)
**Level 5:** Extra Attack (two attacks per turn!)

- Protect the party through preparation

## Tracker: Favored Enemy & Natural Explorer

**Favored Enemy Benefits:**
- Advantage on Survival checks to track
- Advantage on Intelligence checks to recall information
- Language of creature type (if applicable)

**Natural Explorer Benefits:**
- Difficult terrain doesn't slow travel
- Can't become lost except by magical means
- Alert to danger while traveling
- Double proficiency on Intelligence/Wisdom checks related to terrain
