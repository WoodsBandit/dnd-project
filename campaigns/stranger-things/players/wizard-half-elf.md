# Half-Elf Wizard

**Player:** [TBD]
**Race:** Half-Elf
**Class/Subclass:** Wizard (School of Evocation at Level 3)
**Level:** 3 (can advance to Level 5)
**Background:** Sage

## Stranger Things Connection

This character represents **Eleven** from the show. The Half-Elf Wizard embodies Eleven's mysterious psychic powers translated into arcane magic - powerful, otherworldly, and essential to the group's success against supernatural threats.

## Appearance

A studious half-elf devoted to the arcane arts. Half-elves combine human ambition with elven grace, often appearing youthful with slightly pointed ears. Their mixed heritage gives them a unique perspective on both worlds.

## Backstory Summary

The pre-generated characters focus on class, race, and background descriptions rather than extensive individual backstories. As a Sage, this wizard has spent time studying ancient lore and arcane texts, seeking knowledge and power.

## Personality

- **Trait:** Curious about magic and its applications
- **Ideal:** Knowledge - The path to power is through understanding
- **Bond:** Seeks to prove their worth through mastery of the arcane
- **Flaw:** Sometimes overconfident in magical abilities

## Racial Traits (Half-Elf)

- **Ability Score Increase:** +2 Charisma, +1 to two other abilities (likely INT and CON)
- **Darkvision:** 60 feet
- **Fey Ancestry:** Advantage on saves vs. charm, immunity to magical sleep
- **Skill Versatility:** Proficiency in two skills of choice
- **Languages:** Common, Elvish, and one additional language

## Class Features (Wizard Level 3)

- **Spellcasting:** Intelligence-based arcane spellcasting
- **Arcane Recovery:** Recover spell slots on short rest (once per day, regain slots up to level 2)
- **Arcane Tradition - Evocation:** Focus on destructive magic
  - **Evocation Savant:** Copy evocation spells faster/cheaper
  - **Sculpt Spells:** Protect allies from your evocation spells

## Spell Slots (Level 3)
- 1st Level: 4 slots
- 2nd Level: 2 slots

## Combat Stats (Estimated)

- **Armor Class:** 12 (15 with Mage Armor, 16 with Ring of Protection)
- **Hit Points:** ~16 (6 + 2d6 + CON modifier x 3)
- **Speed:** 30 feet
- **Proficiency Bonus:** +2

## Role in the Party

The Wizard serves as:
- **Blaster:** Powerful offensive spells (Evocation focus)
- **Controller:** Sleep, crowd control options
- **Utility:** Ritual casting, Detect Magic
- **Knowledge:** Arcana and Investigation checks

## Combat Tactics

1. **Opening:** Cast Mage Armor if expecting combat
2. **Positioning:** Stay behind the Paladin and Ranger
3. **Sculpt Spells:** Use AoE spells safely around allies
4. **Resource Management:** Save spell slots for important fights
5. **Fire:** Essential against the Thessalhydra's regeneration

### Key Combat Spells by Situation

| Situation | Best Spell |
|-----------|------------|
| Single target | Scorching Ray, Magic Missile |
| Groups of enemies | Burning Hands (sculpt around allies) |
| Emergency defense | Shield (reaction) |
| Escape | Misty Step |
| Thessalhydra | Fire spells to prevent regeneration |

## Important: Protect the Spellbook!

The Wizard's spellbook is essential. Without it, they cannot prepare new spells. Keep it in a Bag of Holding for safety.

## Leveling Notes

The adventure allows advancement to Level 5:

**Level 4:**
- Ability Score Improvement (+2 INT recommended)
- New spells

**Level 5:**
- 3rd Level Spells (Fireball!)
- Additional spell slot

## Goals

### Short-term
- Unleash arcane power against the Thessalhydra
- Support the party with fire magic (prevents regeneration)
- Stay alive (low HP!)

### Long-term
- Master the arcane arts
- Prove worthy of the party's trust
