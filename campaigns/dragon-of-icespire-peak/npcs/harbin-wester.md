# Harbin Wester

**Race/Ancestry:** Human
**Occupation:** Townmaster of Phandalin
**Location:** Townmaster's Hall, Phandalin
**Faction:** Phandalin Government
**Status:** Alive

## Appearance

[FILL FROM BOOK - Physical description]

Notably wears a cloak made of metallic silver material that he believes will magically protect him from dragon lightning (it doesn't provide any actual protection).

## Personality

- **Trait:** Paranoid and terrified; rarely leaves his hall except to post quests and get food
- **Ideal:** Safety above all else
- **Bond:** Phandalin (though he protects it from behind closed doors)
- **Flaw:** Cowardice--other townsfolk openly consider him a coward

### Behavioral Notes
- Has been hiding even before the dragon attacked
- Relies entirely on adventurers to solve problems
- Will not take direct action himself under any circumstances
- Communicates primarily through job board postings

## Voice/Mannerisms

- Nervous, stammering speech
- Constantly looks over his shoulder
- Flinches at loud noises
- Speaks through partially-opened doors when possible
- Clutches his "protective" cloak when stressed

## Relationship to Party

- **First Met:** Session 1, at Townmaster's Hall
- **Disposition:** Desperately friendly (needs their help)
- **Owes/Owed:** Owes quest rewards; relies on party for town's survival

## What They Know

- Location of all job board quests (he posts them)
- General knowledge of threats facing Phandalin
- Information about the white dragon sightings
- Names and locations of various NPCs who need help
- Limited practical knowledge (secondhand information)

## What They Want

- The dragon threat eliminated so he can stop hiding
- Adventurers to solve every problem so he doesn't have to
- To appear competent as townmaster without taking risks
- Eventually: to throw a feast in the heroes' honor once Cryovain is defeated

## Quest Connections

Harbin is the primary quest-giver for the job board system:

### Starting Quests (Level 1)
- **Dwarven Excavation** - 50 gp reward
- **Gnomengarde** - Hopes gnomes have magic items to help against dragon
- **Umbrage Hill** - Concerned about Adabra Gwynn's safety

### Follow-Up Quests (Level 3)
- **Butterskull Ranch** - 100 gp reward (or proof of Big Al's death)
- **Loggers' Camp** - 100 gp reward
- **Mountain's Toe Gold Mine** - Escort mission for Don-Jon Raskin

### Advanced Quests (Level 4-5)
- **Axeholm** - Clear the dwarven fortress as evacuation refuge

## Story Arc

1. **Introduction:** Cowering in Townmaster's Hall, desperately posting quests
2. **Mid-Adventure:** Attempts to evacuate villagers to Axeholm (if cleared)
3. **Resolution:** Once Cryovain is defeated, emerges from hiding and throws a feast in the heroes' honor
