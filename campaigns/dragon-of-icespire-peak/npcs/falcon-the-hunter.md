# Falcon the Hunter

**Race/Ancestry:** Human (presumed)
**Occupation:** Hunter / Lodge Keeper / Guide for Nobility
**Location:** Falcon's Hunting Lodge, Neverwinter Wood
**Faction:** Independent
**Status:** Alive

## Appearance

[FILL FROM BOOK - Physical description]

Runs a hunting lodge deep in Neverwinter Wood that caters to nobles from Neverwinter seeking guided hunts.

## Personality

- **Trait:** Professional and polished; accustomed to dealing with nobility
- **Ideal:** Hospitality and expert service
- **Bond:** His hunting lodge and reputation among Neverwinter's elite
- **Flaw:** Prioritizes his business over adventure; won't leave the lodge

### Behavioral Notes
- Polite but firm in his refusals to join adventures
- Expert knowledge of the Neverwinter Wood
- Maintains the lodge as a sanctuary in the wilderness
- Professional demeanor befitting someone who serves nobles

## Voice/Mannerisms

- Refined speech (deals with nobility regularly)
- Practical and knowledgeable about the wilderness
- Calm and measured responses
- Politely declines any offer that would take him from the lodge

## Relationship to Party

- **First Met:** Falcon's Hunting Lodge (prerequisite for Woodland Manse quest)
- **Disposition:** Friendly; appreciates capable adventurers
- **Owes/Owed:** Offers Boots of Elvenkind as reward for Woodland Manse quest

## What They Know

- Extensive knowledge of Neverwinter Wood
- Location and nature of the Woodland Manse
- Information about the anchorites threatening the area
- Hunting and tracking expertise
- Noble clients and their interests

## What They Want

- The anchorite threat to his lodge eliminated
- To continue running his business for Neverwinter nobles
- His lodge protected as a sanctuary in the dangerous woods
- Capable adventurers to handle the threats he cannot

## Quest Connection: Woodland Manse

### Quest Description
[FILL FROM BOOK - Exact quest text]

General summary: "The orcs have fallen under the sway of evil spellcasters in Neverwinter Wood, and have been sighted in growing numbers near Falcon's Hunting Lodge. The spellcasters dwell in a ruined manse. Falcon needs someone to make a preemptive strike against it."

### Adventure Flow
1. Party takes Woodland Manse quest from job board
2. Must visit **Falcon's Hunting Lodge** first
3. Falcon provides information about the ruined manse
4. Party proceeds to assault the Woodland Manse
5. After manse is cleared, potential counterattack on the lodge

### The Lodge as Base
- Falcon's Hunting Lodge can serve as a wilderness base of operations
- Provides safe rest in Neverwinter Wood
- Falcon won't join the party but offers hospitality

### Reward
If the party is eligible for and completes the Woodland Manse quest:
- **Boots of Elvenkind** (wondrous item, uncommon)
  - Steps make no sound regardless of surface
  - Advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks relying on moving silently

## Post-Adventure

After Cryovain is defeated:
- Falcon returns to normal operations at his lodge
- May become a recurring NPC for wilderness adventures
- Can provide information about the woods for future quests
