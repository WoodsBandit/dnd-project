## King Korboz

**Race/Ancestry:** Rock Gnome
**Occupation:** King of Gnomengarde (co-ruler)
**Location:** Gnomengarde (barricaded in royal chambers)
**Faction:** Gnomengarde
**Status:** Alive (insane)

### Situation
- Recently went mad after a traumatic encounter
- Believes a "shapechanger" is lurking in Gnomengarde (he's right--it's a mimic)
- Has locked himself in the bedchamber with Gnerkli
- Has glued King Gnerkli to a chair for "safekeeping"
- His madness is the central mystery of the Gnomengarde quest

### The Truth
- Korboz witnessed or experienced something related to the mimic
- His paranoia about a shapechanger is actually correct
- Curing him requires dealing with the mimic threat

### Personality
- Paranoid and fearful
- Protective of Gnerkli (in his twisted way)
- Was presumably reasonable before the madness

## Fibblestib Pinebark

**Race/Ancestry:** Rock Gnome
**Occupation:** Chief Inventor
**Location:** Gnomengarde
**Faction:** Gnomengarde
**Status:** Alive

### Role
- One of Gnomengarde's foremost inventors
- Works alongside Dabbledob Plasterpatch
- Highly respected and trusted pillar of the community
- Devoted to the two kings

### The Cure Debate
Fibblestib and Dabbledob have reached an impasse about curing King Korboz:
- **Fibblestib's Solution:** Invent a "sanity ray"
- **Dabbledob's Response:** Dismisses it as "stupid"

### Personality
- Innovative and creative
- Focused on the kings' wellbeing
- Can be somewhat indifferent to other gnomes' problems
- Argumentative with Dabbledob despite their partnership

### Gender Note
Fibblestib is identified as male in Dragon of Icespire Peak but as female in the sequel Divine Contention. The wiki uses male based on the primary sourcebook.

## The Mimic

**Race/Ancestry:** Mimic (aberration/monstrosity)
**Occupation:** Predator
**Location:** Gnomengarde (hiding)
**Faction:** None
**Status:** Alive (until killed by party)

### Role in Quest
- The true source of King Korboz's madness
- Has been killing gnomes secretly
- Can be run as an Alien/The Thing style hunt
- Creates paranoia among the gnome population

### Running the Mimic
- Can stretch out the mystery
- Have gnomes disappear or be found dead
- Build paranoia and tension
- The mimic can hide as various objects
- Consider having NPCs be suspicious of each other
