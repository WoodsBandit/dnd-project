## Background

### Career History
- Former sheriff of Triboar (town to the east)
- Known as "Big Al Kalazorn" during his law enforcement days
- Retired about a decade ago
- Retirement didn't suit him--needed a new challenge

### The Ranch
- Claimed fertile land five miles east of Conyberry
- Built it into a cattle and horse ranch
- Later added: pig farm, chicken coops, vegetable gardens, corn fields, apple orchard
- Famous for **butter skulls**--butter molded into humanoid skull shapes
- Sells primarily to towns east, some reach Barthen's Provisions in Phandalin
- Butter made from milk of his prized cow, Petunia

## Quest: Butterskull Ranch

### Quest Description
[FILL FROM BOOK - Exact quest text from job board]

General summary: "Orcs have attacked Butterskull Ranch, five miles east of Conyberry along the Triboar Trail! Travel there with haste, assess the damage, and help any way you can. Ranch owner Alfonse 'Big Al' Kalazorn is a retired sheriff who can reward you for your efforts. If he's dead, return to Townmaster Harbin Wester with proof of Kalazorn's demise to receive a reward of 100 gp."

### The Situation
- Orc attack about a tenday ago
- Orcs were driven from Icespire Hold by Cryovain
- They freed the pigs, burned the barn and smithy
- Big Al was captured; ranch hands were killed
- One hand escaped on horseback to Phandalin with the news
- Big Al is tied up in the cellar among his butter skulls

### Key NPC: Petunia
- Big Al's prized dairy cow
- Source of his famous butter skull butter
- His primary concern even while captive
- Saving her earns his deep gratitude

### Combat Considerations
- Recommended: 3 orcs per character (not counting sidekicks)
- Spread orcs across the ranch to avoid overwhelming parties
- Adjust numbers based on party size and capability
- Important to convey WHY orcs are here (displaced by Cryovain)

### Resolution & Reward
- Finding Big Al in the cellar
- **Mithral Chain Mail** (rare armor, given as reward)
  - No Strength requirement
  - No disadvantage on Stealth checks
  - Appears as normal clothing
