**Race/Ancestry:** Human
**Occupation:** Midwife / Apothecary / Acolyte of Chauntea
**Location:** Umbrage Hill (stone windmill)
**Faction:** None (independent)
**Status:** Alive

## Personality

- **Trait:** Stubbornly independent; refuses to abandon her home despite danger
- **Ideal:** Service to the community through healing
- **Bond:** Her windmill home and her work as midwife/healer
- **Flaw:** Stubborn to the point of endangering herself

### Behavioral Notes
- Will not return to Phandalin regardless of how dangerous things get
- Practical and no-nonsense approach to problems
- Devoted to Chauntea, goddess of agriculture and life
- Provides healing services to the surrounding area

## Voice/Mannerisms

- Matter-of-fact tone
- Calm under pressure (deals with childbirth and medical emergencies)
- Dismissive of what she considers unnecessary worry
- Warm but firm

## Relationship to Party

- **First Met:** Umbrage Hill quest
- **Disposition:** Grateful if saved from manticore; friendly thereafter
- **Owes/Owed:** Gives potion of healing as thanks for dealing with manticore

## What They Know

- Local herbal remedies and healing knowledge
- Information about the surrounding wilderness
- Manticore behavior and habits (from recent harassment)
- General knowledge of Phandalin and its people
- Lore of Chauntea and nature spirits

## What They Want

- To be left alone to continue her work
- The manticore threat dealt with (but won't leave even if it isn't)
- Recognition that she can take care of herself
- To continue serving the community as healer/midwife

## Quest: Umbrage Hill

### Quest Description
[FILL FROM BOOK - Exact quest text from job board]

General summary: "The local midwife, an acolyte of Chauntea named Adabra Gwynn, lives by herself in a stone windmill on the side of a hill a few miles south of Phandalin. With dragon sightings becoming more common, it's not safe for her to be alone. Urge Adabra to return to Phandalin."

### The Situation
When the party arrives, a **manticore** is harassing Adabra at her windmill. The encounter can be resolved through:
- Combat (defeating the manticore)
- Diplomacy (the manticore is intelligent and can be reasoned with)

### Resolution Options
1. **Kill the manticore:** Adabra is grateful, gives reward
2. **Drive off/negotiate with manticore:** Also acceptable resolution
3. **Complete the quest:** Adabra declines to return to Phandalin but provides a note for Harbin Wester confirming she's safe

### Rewards
- **Potion of Healing** (given by Adabra for dealing with manticore)
- Note for Harbin Wester (completes quest)
- Potential ongoing relationship with a healer NPC
