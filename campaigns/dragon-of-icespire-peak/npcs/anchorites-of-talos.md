# Anchorites of Talos

This file covers the antagonist NPCs associated with the Talos cult.

---

## Overview

The **Anchorites of Talos** are half-orc wereboars dedicated to the worship of Talos, the evil god of storms. They represent one of the main villain factions in Dragon of Icespire Peak.

### Faction Goals
- Spread chaos in the name of Talos
- Summon Gorthok the Thunder Boar to unleash destruction
- Control territory in Neverwinter Wood
- Perform sacrificial rituals at the Circle of Thunder

### Key Ability
The anchorites can transform into boars (wereboar lycanthropy).

- If Moesko is killed but his heart survives, he resurrects in 24 hours

## Gorthok the Thunder Boar

**Race/Ancestry:** Primal Spirit (manifests as giant boar)
**Occupation:** Servant of Talos / Destructive Force
**Location:** Summoned at Circle of Thunder
**Faction:** Servant of Talos
**Status:** Can be summoned in stormy weather

### Appearance
A shadowy primal spirit that appears as an elephant-sized boar with lightning dancing along its tusks and a gleam of malice in its eyes.

### Summoning Requirements
- Must be stormy weather
- Ritual performed at the Circle of Thunder
- Standing stones help focus the anchorites' magic
- Once summoned, can be unleashed against enemies

### Combat
- Elephant-sized (massive creature)
- Lightning-based attacks (tusks crackle with electricity)
- Primal fury and malice

## Talos the Storm Lord

**Domain:** Storms, destruction, chaos
**Alignment:** Chaotic Evil
**Symbol:** Three lightning bolts radiating from a point
**Followers:** Those who embrace destruction and fear storms

### Worship
- Followers seek to spread chaos
- Storms are sacred manifestations of Talos's power
- Sacrifices made to appease or empower the Storm Lord
- The Tower of Storms serves as both temple and trap
