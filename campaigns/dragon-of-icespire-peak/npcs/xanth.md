## Background

### The Displacement
When Cryovain arrived in the Sword Mountains:
1. The dragon drove orcs from Icespire Hold
2. Many orcs sought refuge with the anchorites of Talos at the Circle of Thunder
3. The newly arrived orcs drove Xanth from his home
4. Xanth fled west toward the High Road to seek help

### Chain of Displacement
- Cryovain displaces orcs from Icespire Hold
- Orcs flee to Circle of Thunder
- Orcs + anchorites displace Xanth
- Xanth seeks adventurers for help

## Quest Connection: Circle of Thunder

### How Party Meets Xanth
1. **Dragon Barrow route:** Characters who brave Dragon Barrow might encounter and travel with Xanth
2. **Random encounter:** May find Xanth on roads near Neverwinter Wood
3. **Seeking help:** Xanth actively looks for capable warriors

### Circle of Thunder Information
Xanth can share:
- Location of the Circle of Thunder
- Number and nature of the anchorites
- The orcs who joined them
- Gorthok the Thunder Boar (if he knows)
- Best approaches to the location

### As a Guide
- Can lead party through Neverwinter Wood
- Knows safe paths and dangerous areas

- Combat capability as ally
