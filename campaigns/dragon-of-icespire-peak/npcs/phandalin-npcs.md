# Phandalin NPCs

This file covers NPCs found in the town of Phandalin who are not the primary quest-givers.

---

## Toblen Stonehill

**Race/Ancestry:** Human
**Occupation:** Innkeeper
**Location:** Stonehill Inn, Phandalin
**Faction:** Phandalin Townsfolk
**Status:** Alive

### Role
- Owner and operator of the Stonehill Inn
- Primary source of rumors and local gossip
- Runs the inn with his wife Trilena and son Pip

### Background
- Arrived in Phandalin from Triboar early in the town's reconstruction
- Founded the inn around 1490 DR
- The inn is one of the largest buildings in town

### Services
- Rooms: 5 sp per night
- Food and ale at standard prices
- Meals approximately 1 sp

### What They Know
- Local rumors (can lead to Tower of Storms and other side quests)
- Town gossip and recent events
- Information about visitors like Don-Jon Raskin
- General Sword Coast news

## Elmar Barthen

**Race/Ancestry:** Human
**Occupation:** Shopkeeper
**Location:** Barthen's Provisions, Phandalin
**Faction:** Phandalin Townsfolk
**Status:** Alive

### Appearance
Lean and balding, middle-aged man with a kindly manner.

### Role
- Runs Barthen's Provisions, the biggest trading post in Phandalin
- Open from sunup to sundown
- Sells general adventuring supplies

### Connection to Other NPCs
- Sells some of Big Al Kalazorn's butter skulls
- General supplier for the town

### Services
Standard adventuring equipment and supplies at PHB prices.

## Daran Edermath

**Race/Ancestry:** Half-Elf
**Occupation:** Retired Adventurer / Orchardist
**Location:** Edermath Orchard, Phandalin
**Faction:** Order of the Gauntlet (retired)
**Status:** Alive

### Appearance
Silver-haired half-elf living in a simple cottage.

### Background
- Retired marshal from the lands of the Dragon Coast
- Returned to the Neverwinter region (his original home) to retire
- Grows apples and makes cider

### Role
- Source of adventuring wisdom
- May have knowledge of old dangers
- Represents the Order of the Gauntlet connection

### What They Know
- Adventuring experience and lore
- History of the region
- Potential threats and old legends

---

## Sister Garaele

**Race/Ancestry:** Elf
**Occupation:** Priestess / Harper Agent
**Location:** Shrine of Luck, Phandalin
**Faction:** Harpers
**Status:** Alive

### Role
- Zealous young elf tending the Shrine of Luck
- Shrine dedicated to Tymora, goddess of luck
- Harper agent (secret faction allegiance)

### The Shrine
- Small shrine made of stone from the ruins
- Phandalin's only temple
- Dedicated to Tymora

### What They Know
- Religious knowledge
- Harper intelligence (if trusted)
- Blessing of Tymora available
