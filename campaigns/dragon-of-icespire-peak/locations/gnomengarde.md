# Gnomengarde

**Type:** Gnome Settlement (underground)
**Region:** Sword Mountains
**Population:** Small community of rock gnomes
**Government:** Two co-ruling Kings (Gnerkli and Korboz)

## Description

Gnomengarde is a small kingdom of rock gnomes carved into caves within the Sword Mountains. Known for their inventions and magical items, the gnomes are reclusive and secretive but may provide aid against the dragon threat.

### Quest Hook
Harbin Wester hopes the gnomes have magic items that could help against Cryovain. The party is sent to request their aid.

## The Central Mystery

### The Problem
- King Korboz has gone mad
- He's locked himself in the royal chambers
- He's imprisoned King Gnerkli "for safekeeping"
- The gnome community is in crisis

### The Truth
- A **mimic** has infiltrated Gnomengarde
- It has been killing gnomes secretly
- Korboz witnessed something and correctly believes there's a shapechanger
- His paranoia is justified but his response is extreme

### Running the Mystery
Consider running this as an Alien/The Thing style hunt:
- Build paranoia among the gnome NPCs
- Have gnomes disappear or be found dead
- NPCs suspect each other
- The mimic can hide as various objects
- Stretch out the mystery for tension
