# Umbrage Hill

**Type:** Wilderness Location (windmill on hill)
**Region:** South of Phandalin
**Population:** 1 (Adabra Gwynn)
**Government:** N/A

## Description

Umbrage Hill is a small rise a few miles south of Phandalin, notable for the stone windmill where Adabra Gwynn, the local midwife and apothecary, lives alone.

### Quest Level
Recommended for 1st level characters. One of three Starting Quests.

## Points of Interest

### The Stone Windmill
- **What:** Adabra Gwynn's home and apothecary
- **Who:** Adabra Gwynn (midwife, acolyte of Chauntea)
- **Hooks:** Rescue from manticore; convince to return to Phandalin (she refuses)

### The Hill
- **What:** Elevated terrain providing views of surrounding area
- **Who:** Currently harassed by a manticore
- **Hooks:** Tactical terrain for manticore encounter
