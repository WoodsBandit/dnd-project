# Dwarven Excavation

**Type:** Archaeological Dig / Ancient Ruins
**Region:** Mountains southwest of Phandalin
**Population:** Dwarf prospectors (at risk)
**Government:** N/A

## Description

An archaeological dig site in the mountains southwest of Phandalin where dwarf prospectors have discovered ancient dwarven ruins. They've been excavating, seeking treasure and relics from their ancestors.

### Quest Level
Recommended for 1st level characters. One of three Starting Quests.
