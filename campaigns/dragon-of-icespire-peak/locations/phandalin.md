# Phandalin

**Type:** Frontier Town
**Region:** Sword Coast North, foothills of the Sword Mountains
**Population:** Small (exact number varies by source)
**Government:** Townmaster (Harbin Wester)

## Description

Phandalin is a small frontier mining town nestled in the foothills of the Sword Mountains. The town features stone buildings with wooden roofs lining dirt streets. Though humble, Phandalin has been growing since its reconstruction and recovery from the Redbrand bandit troubles.

### State in Dragon of Icespire Peak
Unlike in Lost Mine of Phandelver, Phandalin in this adventure is:
- Post-Redbrand (bandits already dealt with)
- Growing and recovering
- Threatened by new external dangers (dragon, displaced monsters)
- Under the fearful "leadership" of Harbin Wester

## Points of Interest

### Townmaster's Hall
- **What:** Municipal building with job board, small jail in cellar
- **Who:** Harbin Wester (townmaster)
- **Hooks:** Job board is the primary quest source; three quests available at a time

### Stonehill Inn
- **What:** Two-story inn, one of the largest buildings in town
- **Who:** Toblen Stonehill (owner), Trilena (wife), Pip (son)
- **Hooks:** Rumors leading to side quests (Tower of Storms, etc.); Don-Jon Raskin stays here before escort quest
- **Services:** Rooms 5 sp/night, standard food and drink prices

### Barthen's Provisions
- **What:** Biggest trading post in town, open sunup to sundown
- **Who:** Elmar Barthen (lean, balding, kindly)
- **Hooks:** General supplies; sells some of Big Al's butter skulls
- **Services:** Standard adventuring equipment

### Lionshield Coster
- **What:** Weapons and armor trading post
- **Who:** Linene Graywind (sharp-tongued), Minghee (daughter)
- **Hooks:** Martial equipment needs
- **Services:** Weapons, armor, shields

### Shrine of Luck
- **What:** Small stone shrine to Tymora, only temple in town
- **Who:** Sister Garaele (elf priestess, Harper agent)
- **Hooks:** Blessings, Harper connection, religious services

### Edermath Orchard
- **What:** Apple orchard with cottage
- **Who:** Daran Edermath (silver-haired half-elf, retired adventurer)
- **Hooks:** Adventuring wisdom, Order of the Gauntlet connection, local lore

### Phandalin Miner's Exchange
- **What:** Trading post for miners to weigh and sell finds

- **Hooks:** Mining information, potential rumors about mountains

### The Sleeping Giant
- **What:** Rundown, dangerous tavern
- **Who:** Grista Kettlecopp (surly dwarf)

### Tresendar Manor
- **What:** Ancient ruined manor/castle on hill overlooking town
