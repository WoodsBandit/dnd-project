### Common Failure Types
- **Adhesion** - Didn't stick to bed
- **Spaghetti** - Print came loose mid-print
- **Layer shift** - Layers misaligned
- **Stringing** - Excessive stringing
- **Supports** - Support failure
- **Warping** - Part warped/curled
- **Clog** - Nozzle clogged
- **Resin** - Resin-specific failure
