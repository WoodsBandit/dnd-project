# Troglodyte Caverns - 3D Terrain Design Specification

## Overview

Modular terrain system for an 11-area Troglodyte dungeon encounter. Designed for FDM 3D printing with 0.2mm layer height, minimal/no supports, and tabletop gaming durability.

**Scale:** 28mm heroic (1" = 5 feet in-game)
**Grid:** 1" squares for D&D/Pathfinder compatibility
**Base Height:** 3mm for all floor pieces

---

## Connection System

### Primary Connection: OpenLOCK Clip System
- **Clip Channels:** 10mm wide x 6mm deep x 20mm long
- **Clip Size:** Standard OpenLOCK clips (freely available STL)
- **Channel Placement:** Centered on each edge, 0.5" from corners
- **Tolerance:** 0.2mm gap for easy insertion/removal

### Secondary Connection: Magnetic Option
- **Magnet Size:** 6mm x 3mm disc magnets
- **Pocket Depth:** 2.8mm (slight interference fit)
- **Polarity:** North-up on all pieces (universal orientation)
- **Placement:** Corner pockets, 5mm from edges

### Friction Fit Alternative
- **Nub System:** 3mm hemisphere on one edge, matching depression on opposite
- **Use Case:** Quick setup without clips/magnets

## FDM Print Optimization Guide

### Recommended Settings

| Parameter | Value | Notes |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| Layer Height | 0.2mm | Balance of detail/speed |
| Nozzle | 0.4mm | Standard |
| Walls | 3-4 | Durability |
| Top/Bottom | 3 layers | Solid surfaces |
| Infill | 15-20% | Weight/strength balance |
| Speed | 50mm/s | Quality priority |
| Temperature | Material-specific | PLA: 200-210C |
| Bed Temp | 60C | PLA on glass/PEI |

### Support-Free Design Principles

1. **Maximum Overhang:** 45 degrees from vertical
2. **Bridge Length:** Maximum 20mm unsupported
3. **Stalactites:** Design with 50-degree taper
4. **Arches:** Segment into printable angles
5. **Horizontal Holes:** Teardrop shape or segment top

### Orientation Guidelines

| Piece Type | Orientation | Reason |
|------------|-------------|--------|
| Floor Tiles | Face up | Texture quality |
| Walls | On side | No supports needed |
| Archways | Upright | Structural integrity |
| Accessories | Varies | Minimize supports |

### Post-Processing

1. **Support Removal:** N/A (support-free design)
2. **Layer Lines:** Light sanding or leave for texture
3. **Stringing:** Heat gun quick pass
4. **Warping Prevention:** Brim on large flat pieces

## Painting Guide

### Recommended Paint Scheme

#### Base Colors (Primer First - Grey or Black)

| Element | Base Color | Wash | Drybrush |
|---------|------------|------|----------|
| Stone | Dark Grey | Black/Nuln Oil | Light Grey |
| Bone | Ivory/Cream | Sepia/Agrax | White |
| Wood | Brown | Dark Brown | Tan |
| Metal | Gunmetal | Black | Silver |
| Webs | Off-White | Light Grey | Pure White |
| Water | Dark Blue-Green | Blue | White (edges) |
| Acid | Yellow-Green | Green | Yellow |
| Blood | Dark Red | Brown | N/A (gloss varnish) |

### Technique Tips

**Stone Texture:**
1. Prime grey
2. Heavy black wash in recesses
3. Drybrush medium grey
4. Light drybrush pale grey on edges
5. Optional: Moss green in crevices

**Wet Effects:**
1. Gloss varnish for water/slime
2. Water effects medium for pools
3. UV resin for thick liquid (acid)

**Glow Effects (Runes):**
1. Paint rune channels black
2. Apply fluorescent/glow paint
3. Edge highlight with white
4. Consider UV-reactive paint

### Speed Painting Method

1. Zenithal prime (black base, white from above)
2. Single contrast paint coat
3. Drybrush highlights
4. Wash recesses
5. Seal with matte varnish

## Storage and Stacking System

### Tile Stacking Design

**Registration System:**
- 4 nubs on tile bottom (2mm diameter, 1mm tall)
- 4 matching depressions on tile top
- Offset from center to prevent rotation issues

**Stack Height:** 20 tiles stable without container

### Storage Container Recommendations

**Small Box (Floor Tiles):**
- Internal: 110mm x 110mm x 80mm
- Holds: 20 floor tiles + clips

**Medium Box (Walls):**
- Internal: 120mm x 60mm x 120mm
- Holds: 20 wall sections

**Large Box (Set Pieces):**
- Internal: 200mm x 200mm x 100mm
- Foam insert with custom cutouts

### Printable Storage Solutions

**Modular Drawer System:**
- Stackable drawers (print in place hinges)
- Label slots on front
- Interlocking stack design

**Clip Storage:**
- Small box with dividers
- Holds 50+ OpenLOCK clips
- Magnetic lid option

### Transport Considerations

- Foam-lined toolbox recommended
- Separate delicate pieces (stalactites, webs)
- Magnetic bases prevent shifting
- Label containers by dungeon area
